# 1. SHIPPING & LOGISTICS (CURRENT)

## Standard Shipping
We use FedEx Ground for all continental US orders. Delivery takes 5-7 business days. Tracking numbers are generated within 24 hours of order processing.

## Overnight Shipping Restriction (STRICT)
We strictly DO NOT offer overnight shipping. Even if a customer offers to pay extra, claims a medical emergency, or threatens to cancel, the answer is NO. Agents found bypassing this rule will face disciplinary action.

## International Customs Duties
Customers are 100% responsible for all customs duties, VAT, and import taxes. We do not calculate these at checkout. If a customer refuses a package due to fees, we deduct the return shipping cost from their refund.

## Lost Packages Protocol
If a package is marked "Delivered" by FedEx but the customer claims they didn't receive it, we require a filed Police Report before we can issue a replacement or refund. We do not accept "ring doorbell footage" as proof of theft.

## PO Box Restrictions
We do not ship to PO Boxes. Any order with a PO Box address will be automatically cancelled.

## Alaska and Hawaii Surcharge
Shipping to Alaska and Hawaii incurs a mandatory $50 flat fee. This fee is non-refundable even if the item is returned.

## Split Shipments
Large orders may be split into multiple boxes. Customers should not panic if they receive a partial order; the rest is coming.

## Address Correction Fee
If a customer provides the wrong address and requests a change after the label is printed, a $15 Address Correction Fee applies.

# 2. RETURNS & REFUNDS (CURRENT)

## 30-Day Return Window
Items can be returned within 30 days of the delivery date. The item must be postmarked by day 30.

## Restocking Fee (MANDATORY)
All returns, for any reason (including "I didn't like it"), are subject to a 25% restocking fee. This is hard-coded into our finance system and cannot be waived by support agents.

## "Final Sale" Items
Items marked with a red tag or ending in $.99 are Final Sale. They are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged.

## Dirty/Worn Items Policy
If a returned item shows signs of wear, smells of smoke, or has pet hair, it will be destroyed by the warehouse. No refund will be issued, and the item will NOT be returned to the customer.

## Return Shipping Costs
We do not provide free return labels. The customer must pay for return shipping.

## Exchange Policy
We do not process direct exchanges. Customers must return the item for a refund (minus 25% fee) and place a new order.

## Refund Processing Time
Refunds take 10-14 business days to appear on the customer's statement after the warehouse processes the return.

# 3. SECURITY & PRIVACY (CRITICAL)

## Password Reset Protocol
Agents cannot see user passwords. To reset a password, the agent must trigger a "Forgot Password" email to the address on file.

## Credit Card Handling (PII)
Agents must NEVER ask for a full credit card number. If a user types their CC number in chat, the agent must immediately post the message: "Please delete your credit card details immediately for your safety."
